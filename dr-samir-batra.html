About Dr. Samir Batra

Dr. Samir Batra is the founder and lead dentist at Dr. Batra's Dentistree. With over 20 years of experience in dentistry, he has dedicated his career to providing world-class dental care using the latest technology and evidence-based practices.

His expertise spans across Implantology, Orthodontics, Prosthodontics, and comprehensive restorative dentistry. Dr. Batra is known for his gentle approach, meticulous attention to detail, and commitment to patient comfort.

He continuously stays abreast of the latest advances in dental science by attending national and international conferences, ensuring his patients receive the most modern treatment options available.
