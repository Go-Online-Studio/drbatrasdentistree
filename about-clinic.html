Welcome

Where Your Smile Comes First

Dr. Batra's Dentistree is Vadodara's premier multi-specialty dental clinic, dedicated to providing world-class dental care in a warm, welcoming environment. Our clinic combines cutting-edge technology with compassionate, personalized care.

Founded by Dr. Samir Batra, our clinic has been serving the community for over 20 years, building trust through consistent excellence, transparency, and a genuine commitment to every patient's well-being.

From routine check-ups to advanced implant surgeries, we offer comprehensive dental solutions under one roof — all in a safe, hygienic environment that exceeds international standards.
