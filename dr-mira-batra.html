About Dr. Mira Batra

Dr. Mira Batra (MDS — Periodontology and Implantology) is a chief dentist at Dr. Batra's Dentistree. A vivacious and friendly dentist who strongly believes that every client walking out of the clinic should go with a smile on their face.

She has done her BDS and MDS both from Gujarat University, receiving the top position in her speciality. She is a young dynamic dentist with special interest in general, implant and laser dentistry.

She combines her scientific background with an artistic eye to bring the highest quality of personalized care to each of her client. Dr. Mira is a caring, gentle and understanding professional. She continuously keeps abreast of the latest advances and developments in the field of dentistry.
